📌.About Us

Aapani Cha was founded in 2023 by the Patel brothers, who discovered their passion for tea while helping in their family Kirana store in Becharaji. After learning everything from tea tasting to packaging and distribution in 2022, they decided to bring their love of tea to the masses.

Within just two years, Aapani Cha expanded across Gujarat and earned the prestigious ISO 9001:2000 certification in 2024 for maintaining the highest quality standards. Today, Aapani Cha is loved in India and abroad for its authentic taste and premium blends.

  • Premium quality & fresh blends
  • ISO 9001:2000 certified
  • Popular varieties: Yellow, Elaichi, Gold Pattian, Masala, Adrak & Tulsi Tea
